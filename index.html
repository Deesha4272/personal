My Hobbies

Get to know me! These are some of my organizations and hobbies!

WEHack2020

I am organizing the first ever all-female hackathon at UTD. It is the largest hackathon for female and non binary students in North Texas. We are creating a diverse environment to empower women in engineering to help build confidence while developing technical skills!

Women Who Compute - Ladies in Tech Mentor

The ladies in tech mentor program was established to help women in engineering at UTD feel connected to their major and other female engineers. As a mentor to two mentees, I work with them to find a way to achieve their goals and face their challenges. My goal is to engage freshman women engineers and provide mentorship on professional skills (and have fun!).

Society of Women in Engineering UTD

I have been a part of SWE for two years! I am an officer, and I manage a committee of 3 students to organize our annual SWE UTD Conference scholarship to take brilliant women engineers to the largest conference for for women in engineering to listen to empowering keynote speakers in STEM. We collaborate with our sister orgs and ACM (Association of Computing Machinery) and lead workshops on professional development such as resume building, personal websites, and interview tips!

Taekwondo

I am a blackbelt instructor in taekwondo and a part of the World Taekwondo Federation for about 8 years. I enjoy teaching taekwondo self defense classes and summer camps and participate in sparring competitions.

Association of Computing Machinery at UTD (ACM)

ACM is the largest computer science organization at UT Dallas. In ACM Projects, I was a student developer working on a mobile app in a team setting. This was a great experience that not only taught me technical skills, but also important communication and team skills.
